THE SOLUTION

We designed a web app that not only informs the public about upcoming concerts, including dates, locations, and event details, but also enables the pre-sale of artists’ merchandise, making the purchasing process faster and less stressful while letting the public know in advance which items are available.

"Sometimes lines and the inner organization just makes the experience hard to enjoy"

THE CHALLENGE

Due to the high number of concerts in Bogotá, many events tend to be poorly organized and lack sufficient promotion, making it difficult for people to stay informed about upcoming shows.

Another major issue arises when purchasing artist merchandise. Lines are often long, and attendees frequently have no clear idea of what items are available while they are still waiting in line, which can make the experience frustrating and inefficient.
